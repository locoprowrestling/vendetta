Profile

Major Glory is the banner carrier for The Rising — a veteran of Colorado’s independents whose voice can shake the rafters just as hard as his lariats. He rallies the locker room when the odds tilt toward the Pillars and dives into the fight the second someone needs backup.

Every chant of ‘Rise up’ starts with him. Old-school fundamentals blend with street-tough grit, turning his matches into community victories for Longmont’s underdogs.
