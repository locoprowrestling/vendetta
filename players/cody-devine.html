Profile

Cody Devine is the Pillars’ mastermind and a City of Lights Tournament finalist. He enters every arena with three plans, a backup clause, and a willingness to step on anyone to get what he wants.

He’s never unprepared: scouting tape, back-channel calls, and fine-print favors built on old connections. If you think you have him cornered, it’s because he put you there.

Devine treats matches like negotiations. He weaponizes timing, wording, and leverage — and when the paperwork is done, he still has a boot left to drop.
