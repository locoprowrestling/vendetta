Profile

Zeak Gallent walks into the Opera House like a calculated storm. Nobody in the locker room can trace his full story, but every fan in Longmont understands the conviction burning behind his stare.

He rarely speaks about the past. Instead, Zeak channels everything into the present—scouting weaknesses, tightening The Rising’s game plan, and fighting as if their values are the only compass that matters. That focus carried him through the City of Lights Tournament gauntlet and into the winner’s spotlight.

When the bell rings, he becomes proof that devotion can be louder than history—and louder than The Pillars’ contracts, proxies, and power plays.
