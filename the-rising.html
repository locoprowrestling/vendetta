Manifesto

Why We Rise

For the city

Longmont deserves wrestling that sounds like the neighborhoods that show up. The Rising keeps doors open for every fan, every crew member, and every dreamer who stuck around after the cameras left.

For the crew

We invest back into the makers—ring techs, storytellers, photographers, and fighters—who built LoCo Pro Wrestling from garage promos and pop-up gyms.

For the future

The Rising isn’t a moment; it’s a roadmap. Transparent goals, community collabs, and a locker room that has plans for every win.
