LoCo Pro Wrestling brings epic showdown to Dickens Opera House on November 16, 2025

LONGMONT, CO (October 15, 2025) – LoCo Pro Wrestling, Longmont’s home-grown professional wrestling promotion, is thrilled to announce its long-awaited return to the ring with “Battle at the Opera House” at the historic Dickens Opera House on Sunday, November 16, 2025. Doors open at 5:00 PM and the bell rings at 6:00 PM.

After an eight-year hiatus, LoCo Pro Wrestling is transforming the Dickens Opera House at 302 Main Street into a battleground. Fans will experience an unforgettable night of athleticism, drama, and heart-pounding action as heroes and villains collide.

The Rising vs. The Pillars: A struggle for Longmont’s soul

At the heart of the event is the continuing battle for the soul of Longmont between The Rising and The Pillars. The Rising is a movement of wrestlers who fight for justice and change, embodying the hopes of Longmont’s younger generation. They are determined to inspire the community and build a more inclusive future. Opposing them are The Pillars, entrenched forces of control who are determined to keep the spotlight for themselves and maintain the status quo.

This rivalry has been brewing for years. The Pillars represent the old guard, respected but increasingly out of touch, while The Rising challenges the idea that the city’s story is already written. The clash is about more than championships; it’s about who gets to shape Longmont’s future.

Event highlights

  • Tournament-style card: Expect a full slate of singles matches, with winners advancing toward the final round, and the City of Lights Battle Royal determining the third participant in the final.
  • Family-friendly entertainment: The event is suitable for all ages and offers a three-hour program of in-person entertainment.
  • Historic venue: The Dickens Opera House, a 19th-century landmark in downtown Longmont, adds to the drama, providing an intimate atmosphere for high-stakes wrestling.
  • Table-style seating and concessions: Doors open at 5:00 PM with table-style seating. Fans can enjoy food from The Passenger restaurant and drinks throughout the show.

Quotes

“We’re honored to bring LoCo Pro Wrestling back to Longmont after eight long years,” said Aaron Macomber, owner of LoCo Pro Wrestling. “The energy of our hometown crowd is unmatched, and we can’t wait to see the Dickens Opera House become the scene of the ultimate showdown between The Rising and The Pillars. This isn’t just a wrestling event; it’s our community standing up for what it believes in.”

About LoCo Pro Wrestling

LoCo Pro Wrestling is a cinematic, story-driven professional wrestling promotion founded and based in Longmont, Colorado. Born in the underground and raised in the Rockies, LoCo Pro Wrestling blends mythology and mayhem to create compelling narratives that resonate with the local community. With roots deeply planted in Longmont’s culture, LoCo Pro Wrestling aims to provide inclusive entertainment, elevate local talent, and give back to the community that shaped it.

About Dickens Opera House

Constructed in 1881, Dickens Opera House has served as a cultural cornerstone of Longmont for over a century. The venue hosts live music, theatre, weddings, and community events, offering a unique blend of historical charm and modern amenities. Located at 302 Main Street, it continues to provide a gathering place for locals and visitors alike.
