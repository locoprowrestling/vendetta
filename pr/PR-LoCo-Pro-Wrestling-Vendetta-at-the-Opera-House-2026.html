Vendetta at the Opera House 2026 brings Longmont’s rivalry to the historic Dickens Opera House

LONGMONT, CO (January 22, 2026) – LoCo Pro Wrestling, Longmont’s home-grown professional wrestling promotion, announces “Vendetta at the Opera House 2026” at the historic Dickens Opera House on Saturday, March 7, 2026. Doors open at 5:00 PM and the bell rings at 6:15 PM.

LoCo Pro Wrestling returns to Dickens Opera House at 302 Main Street with a night of athleticism, drama, and high-stakes storytelling. Fans can expect a cinematic live event where alliances fracture, momentum shifts, and Longmont’s legacy is put on the line.

The Rising vs. The Pillars: Vendetta takes center stage

The continuing struggle for Longmont’s soul escalates as The Rising and The Pillars collide once more. The Rising fights for change, community, and the future of the city. The Pillars defend tradition, control, and the spotlight they believe they earned. Vendetta is the next chapter in a rivalry that has defined LoCo Pro Wrestling’s return.

When the bell rings, the opera house becomes the proving ground for wrestlers determined to stake their claim on Longmont’s story. Every bout is a statement, and every victory reshapes the balance of power.

Event highlights

  • Story-driven matchups: A full night of singles and showcase bouts that advance the Vendetta narrative.
  • Family-friendly entertainment: A live, in-person show suitable for all ages.
  • Historic venue: The Dickens Opera House provides an intimate setting for high-impact action.
  • Downtown experience: Table-style seating and concessions available on site.

Quote

“LoCo Pro Wrestling is about more than wins and losses — it’s about who this city chooses to be,” said Aaron Macomber, owner of LoCo Pro Wrestling. “Vendetta at the Opera House is the moment where the stakes rise and the stories get real. We’re honored to bring our hometown into the spotlight once again.”

About LoCo Pro Wrestling

LoCo Pro Wrestling is a cinematic, story-driven professional wrestling promotion founded and based in Longmont, Colorado. Born in the underground and raised in the Rockies, LoCo Pro Wrestling blends mythology and mayhem to create compelling narratives that resonate with the local community. With roots deeply planted in Longmont’s culture, LoCo Pro Wrestling aims to provide inclusive entertainment, elevate local talent, and give back to the community that shaped it.

About Dickens Opera House

Constructed in 1881, Dickens Opera House has served as a cultural cornerstone of Longmont for over a century. The venue hosts live music, theatre, weddings, and community events, offering a unique blend of historical charm and modern amenities. Located at 302 Main Street, it continues to provide a gathering place for locals and visitors alike.
