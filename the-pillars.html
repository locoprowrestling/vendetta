Faction Spotlight

The Pillars

Guardians of tradition. Architects of the old order. They hold the contracts, the keys, and the high ground at the Opera House. If The Rising is the spark, The Pillars are the fortress.

Study the history

Doctrine

Order. Tradition. Control.

The corporate spine

From sponsor suites to boardroom bargains, The Pillars bankroll the spectacle and expect deference in return. Every meeting, every match, every moment—preplanned.

Strength through hierarchy

Mentors groom successors. Roles are earned, not given. Locker room order stays tight, and dissent gets handled behind closed doors.

Legacy locked in stone

The Pillars believe Longmont’s story belongs to those who protected it first. They will defend the Opera House tradition, no matter who rushes the stage.

Commandments

How The Pillars operate

Step One

Control the contracts

Every handshake is already notarized. They decide who signs, who climbs, and who watches from the crowd.

Step Two

Protect the brand

Fashioned press releases, curated narratives, and security on standby. The Pillars maintain the perception of control even when the arena shakes.

Step Three

Punish the rebellion

When The Rising pushes too far, The Pillars respond with calculated force—lawsuits, stipulations, or an ambush behind the curtain.

Step Four

Hold the stage

They aim to end every night with the Opera House lights dimming on their crest and their anthem echoing through Main Street.

Swear allegiance.

Secure your seat, memorize the creed, and keep The Rising on a short leash. Order stands as long as The Pillars do.
